Business Logic & Progress Calculations

Achievement System

  • Beginner: 0 activities completed
  • Starter: 1+ activities completed
  • Intermediate: 10+ activities completed
  • Advanced: 25+ activities completed
  • Expert: 50+ activities completed
  • Master: 100+ activities completed

Engagement-Based Subscription

  • Free Plan: Default starting tier
  • Almost Premium: 3+ day streak OR 7+ total activities
  • Premium Earned: 7+ day streak OR 10+ total activities
completion_rate = (completed_entries / total_entries) × 100

Streak Calculation Logic

Calculates consecutive days with completed activities by counting backwards from today where daily_trackers.completion_rate > 0.

SELECT COUNT(*) FROM consecutive_days
WHERE completion_rate > 0
ORDER BY date DESC

Daily Progress Workflow

  • User selects activities for morning/afternoon/evening slots
  • System creates tracker_entries with "pending" status
  • User completes activities (status → "completed")
  • Daily completion_rate automatically recalculated
  • Streaks and subscription status updated in real-time
