Progress Calculation & Business Logic

Achievement Levels (Based on Total Activities)

• Beginner: 0 activities
• Starter: 1+ activities
• Intermediate: 10+ activities
• Advanced: 25+ activities
• Expert: 50+ activities
• Master: 100+ activities

Subscription Tiers (Engagement-Based)

• Free Plan: Default starting tier
• Almost Premium: 3+ day streak OR 7+ activities
• Premium Earned: 7+ day streak OR 10+ activities

Key Formula:
completion_rate = (completed_entries / total_entries) × 100

Streak Calculation

Current streak is calculated by counting consecutive days with completed activities, working backwards from today using the daily_trackers table where completion_rate > 0.

Daily Progress Flow

1. User selects activities for time slots
2. tracker_entries created with "pending" status
3. User marks activities as "completed"
4. completion_rate recalculated in daily_trackers
5. Streaks and achievements updated dynamically
